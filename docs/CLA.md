## Why this exists

Wayland is an open-core project: a freely self-hostable core under AGPL plus a
commercial hosted/Pro tier. The relicense grant in clause 2 is what lets Ferrox
Labs offer the Pro version without forcing every contributor's work into a
proprietary fork, and without having to track down every contributor for
permission later. It is the same model used by GitLab, Grafana, and Sentry.
